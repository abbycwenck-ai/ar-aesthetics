Our Services

Book a Service
01
HydroFacial

A multi-step facial treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, and delivers a surge of hydration and nutrients into the skin using a unique vortex-fusion technology.

02
Custom Facial

A fully personalized facial designed around your unique skin type and concerns — whether it's acne, aging, sensitivity, or dullness, Amy will tailor every step.

03
Dermaplaning

A gentle physical exfoliation that removes dead skin cells and fine vellus hair (peach fuzz), revealing a brighter, smoother complexion and better product absorption.

04
Lash Lift & Tint

Lift, curl, and darken your natural lashes for weeks of effortless beauty — no extensions, no mascara needed. Wake up with gorgeous lashes every single day.

05
Microneedling

Stimulate your skin's natural collagen production with tiny micro-channels, visibly reducing fine lines, scars, pores, and texture for a smoother, more youthful appearance.

06
Waxing Services

Silky smooth results with professional waxing for brows, face, and body. Amy ensures a comfortable, precise experience every time for long-lasting smoothness.

07
Mommy & Me Facials

A special shared experience for moms and their little ones — bonding over self-care with age-appropriate, gentle treatments for both. The perfect gift or outing for two.
